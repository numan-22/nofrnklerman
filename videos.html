Interview With Norman Finkelstein (1/6)

Norman Gary Finkelstein is an American political scientist, activist, professor, and author. His primary fields of research are the Israeli– Palestinian conflict and the politics of the Holocaust. He is a graduate of Binghamton University and received his Ph.D. in political science at Princeton University
